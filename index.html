Introducing, SlideSync!

What is it?

SlideSync lets you use any device to sync a presentation across multiple platforms and viewers.

How do I use it?

One way is to make your presentation with the tool of your choice (PowerPoint, KeyNote, etc), export to PDF, and simply upload the PDF to SlideSync. Or, if you prefer more control, you can make your presentation in pure HTML5/CSS3 using the awesome library dzSlides. Then, we'll take care of the rest!
